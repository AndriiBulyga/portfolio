About me

background

People ask me: What got you into freelancing? I need to self fund my self and needed a job that could fit in around my life style and still work.

This is why becoming a freelancer was perfect fit for me. I was never a great employee I was an entrepreneur at heart. Working for some one else I always had a hard time with this.

The hardest moment for myself was to go against the grain and do what I wanted. Others were questioning what I was doing. Everyone would give me their opinion whether I wanted it or not. This really makes you question are you doing the right thing, and I’m so glad I never listened to them.
